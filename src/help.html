Help

To upload pictures you are able to click on the box to insert the image which will bring you to a file dialog to upload. Alternatively you can drag and drop your image into the box.

To save please click on the "Save" button located on the right hand side of the page you are editing. A green pop up message will appear if your save was successful, otherwise a red popup with a wanring will appear.

Click on the "Edit Shop" button on the header, then click on "Add New Category" and fill in the required fields.
