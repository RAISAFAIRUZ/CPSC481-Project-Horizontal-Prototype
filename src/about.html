About the Photographer

Hi my name is Luna. I am a photographer based in Canada who wants to share my burning passion for photography. My interest for photography began when I was a young girl, when my grandparents bought me my first digital camera on my 7th birthday. This sparked my hobby turned career for photography. I loved taking pictures of nature, whether it be on trips to my cabin during the summer or snowy days during the winter. Now I never leave without my camera.


My passions today consist of travelling across the world to capture the true essence of nature. I have photographed popular destinations like Banff National Park and Islands around Hawaii. It is on my bucket list to photograph as many destinations around the 7 continents of the world. My work has been featured on popular media such as National Geographic and the New York Times. So join me on this journey of capturing the true beauty of earth.
